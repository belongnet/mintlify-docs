---
title: 'Create product collection'
description: 'Create and launch a digital collection of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) that represents unique products or digital assets'
---


<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Open https://app.belong.net/
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
   Press "Connect Wallet" ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/connect_wallet.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Choose preffered wallet (e.g. MetaMask) ![title]( https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/connect_metamask_wallet.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourth Step">
    Choose/connect your Metamask wallet and press "Next" ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/choose_metamask_wallet.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>   
  <Step title="Fifth Step">
    Press "Connect" to view your address, account balance, activity, and suggest transactions for approval ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/connect_metamask_wallet_2.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sixth Step">  
    Press "Sign" to verify ownership of NFTs by confirming your wallet ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/verify_NFT_ownership_signature_request_metamask_wallet.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Seventh Step">    
    Create an account by entering your username/email, checking the Year Pass/Agreement to EULA and Privacy Policy, and pressing "Submit" ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/register.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Eighth Step">
    Press "Next" to use your location to find people and events nearby ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/onboarding.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ninth Step">
    Press the "Plus" icon to open the action menu ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/plus_icon.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tenth Step">
    Press the "Create NFT Tickets" button to begin creating your own NFT-gated event ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/create_NFT_tickets.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>   
  <Step title="Eleventh Step">
    Press "Create A Community (Hub)" or select an existing Hub (if available) ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/hub_choose.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Twelfth Step">  
    Enter the necessary Hub information and then click the "Create A Hub" button ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/hub_creation.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Thirteenth Step">    
    Complete the desired information for your event, select a cover image, and choose an NFT product image and press "Next Step" button. ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/event_creation_step_2.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourteenth Step">
    Select the desired number of tickets/memberships you want to issue. ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/Number%20of%20ticketsmemberships%20you%20want%20to%20issue.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fifteenth Step">
    Select whether you want your tickets to be resellable or non-transferrable. ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/resell.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sixteenth Step">
    Set the price for your tickets/memberships ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/price.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>   
  <Step title="Seventeenth Step">
    Press "Next Step: Collection Settings" to configure royalties, enable whitelisting, and more ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/more.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Eighteenth Step">
    Drag the knob to set NFT royalty payments (optional) ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/royalties.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Nineteenth Step">
    Enable Whitelist (Discounted pricing for select users/communities, optional) ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/whitelisting.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step> 
  <Step title="Twentieth Step">
    Set the URL the buyer will be redirected to after the transaction is completed ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/redirect.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>       
  <Step title="Step Twenty-One">
    Add question fields for buyers, with responses inscribed to the NFT metadata ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/attribute.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>       
 <Step title="Step Twenty-Two">
    Press "Confirm & Finish" to create smart contract ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/confirm%20and%20finish.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>       
 <Step title="Step Twenty-Three">
    Select "Confirm" in MetaMask to proceed with paying the network fee for creating the NFT event ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/confirm_fee.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>       
 <Step title="Step Twenty-Four">
    Congratulations! Your NFT gated event has been successfully created! ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/success.jpg?raw=true) 
  </Step>  
</Steps>
