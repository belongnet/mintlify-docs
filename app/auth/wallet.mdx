---
title: 'Authorization using MetaMask wallet'
description: 'Register and log in with MetaMask'
---

1.  Open https://app.belong.net/.
2.  Press "Connect Wallet". ![title](https://i.ibb.co/zGBqbF3/connect-wallet.jpg)
3.  Choose preffered wallet (e.g. MetaMask). ![title]( https://i.ibb.co/tH2y4pk/connect-metamask-wallet.jpg)
4.  Choose/conect your Metamask wallet and press "Next". ![title](https://i.ibb.co/t49QzCh/choose-metamask-wallet.jpg)
5.  Press "Connect" to view your address, account balance, activity, and suggest transactions for approval. ![title](https://i.ibb.co/4RJ6xQ1/connect-metamask-wallet-2.jpg)
6.  Press "Sign" to verify ownership of NFTs by confirming your wallet. ![title](https://i.ibb.co/SsnTYn4/verify-NFT-ownership-signature-request-metamask-wallet.jpg)
