---
title: 'Authorization using MetaMask wallet'
description: 'Register and log in with MetaMask'
---
<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Open https://app.belong.net/
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
   Press "Connect Wallet" ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/connect_wallet.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Choose preffered wallet (e.g. MetaMask) ![title]( https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/connect_metamask_wallet.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourth Step">
    Choose/connect your Metamask wallet and press "Next" ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/choose_metamask_wallet.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>   
  <Step title="Fifth Step">
    Press "Connect" to view your address, account balance, activity, and suggest transactions for approval ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/connect_metamask_wallet_2.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sixth Step">  
    Press "Sign" to verify ownership of NFTs by confirming your wallet ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/verify_NFT_ownership_signature_request_metamask_wallet.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Seventh Step">    
    Create an account by entering your username/email, checking the Year Pass/Agreement to EULA and Privacy Policy, and pressing "Submit" ![title](https://github.com/belongnet/mintlify-docs/blob/main/images/register.jpg?raw=true)
  </Step>
</Steps>
